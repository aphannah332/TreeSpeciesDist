Introduction:

Juglans cinerea, commonly known as butternut or white walnut,is a species of walnut native to the eastern United States and southeast Canada. The species is not listed as threatened federally in the US, but is listed as “Special Concern” in Kentucky, “Exploitably Vulnerable” in New York State, and “Threatened” in Tennessee.
